Welcome to Keep Savvy. Here you will find: Affordable Labor, Skilled Workers, Tools for Both!
Here we allow prison management to broadcast the skill of inmates to make better use of their time using their skills to make a wage and become more employable after prison. We also help link employers to affordable labor by scouting workers that would be a good fit within specific companies.

Key Features

Some workers have a hard time finding work. Especially with a history. Good people looking for a second chance and a good company don't miss out let it be you who throws out a hand for hard workers that need to provide for their family.

Finding affordable labor doesn't have to be hard. Especially, if you think outside the box. Prisoners are an untapped labor force. They can provide you with skilled workers that will work for an affordable wage.

We have the tools necessary to help you find honest employers willing to arrange a job for people looking to change their lives. We also have the tools to help you allocate individuals to the right jobs for them both inside and outside of a sentence.
